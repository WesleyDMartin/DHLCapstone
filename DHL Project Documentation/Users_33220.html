Summary

This page summarizes the expected users of our system

This roles are made without knowledge of Digital Human Libraries current user models. This page will likely need to be adjusted based off of how the current user sign up operates.

Learner

A learner will be any teacher/educator/student who is making use of the catalog, and its search functionality, to learn and explore new topics and cultures. A learner can simply sign up and be ready to search. To join

Types

Name

Role

Read Only Learner

A learner who can only search the catalog and view content. They will be able to view a set of videos, as controlled by whatever class or group they are a part of.

Elevated Learner

They will be a learner (likely in the role of teacher or group director) who will have all the capabilities of a read only learner, along with the ability to create a group and invite other learners.

This may be redundant

Submitter

A submitter is a user who helps create the content in the catalog. They are someone who has the ability to upload new videos to the catalog. They are responsible for adding any important location tags, or personalized data which may be useful. Submitters can simply sign up and automatically be ready to submit clips.

Curator

A curator is a user with the responsibility/capability to filter videos from the overall database, based on any business rules regarding disallowed content (adult, violent, etc). Videos added by submitters will need to be approved by curators

Maybe to ease the workload on curators, a verified submitter role could be created?

Administrator

An administrator will be responsible for adding curators to the system, as well as running the system as a whole.
